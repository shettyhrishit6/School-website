Prithvi cafe

I had visited in dec 2018

A go to place situated near the Juhu beach pritvhi cafe is of the most amazing famous and busy cafes of the Mumbai city.Usually crowded and requires atleast 45-60 minutes of waiting in the evening hours the wait is worth it!!the food they serve is amazing and also not too much heavy on the pocket.The ambience and the service is amazing and any amount of praises to the food would be less!You gotta try it yourselves to believe it!!It's damn Delicious.

Jantar mantar

I had visited in feb 2019

Good place. But most of the common people would not be able to understand it's significant, such as me. Only 2km from paharganj.Buses are available. It is a cluster of instruments to measure the location and movement of celestial bodies such as the sun, the moon, other planets and stars in different time of the year. And to measure time seeing the shadow by day.
